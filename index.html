About Us

KTH Hyperloop was founded in April 2018 as Scandinavia's first student team working on bringing the Hyperloop concept to life. The team currently consists of 50+ students with expertise in the fields of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, systems and control engineering, computer science, aerospace engineering, business, and marketing.

  • Our aim this year is to build a fully functional pod!
  • We are aiming to showcase our pod at the European Hyperloop Week this summer

Hydro Extrusions is part of the Norway-based Hydro group and is a world-leading player in extrusion of aluminum profiles. The business area has about 100 production facilities in 40 countries and employs 21,000 people. Through our unique combination of local expertise, global networks and unmatched R&D capacity, we can offer everything from standard profiles to advanced development and manufacturing for most industries. Technology & innovation​ are one our key focus areas and therefore it is natural for us to support the Hyperloop project.

Accelerating Hyperloop Through Simulations

MSC Software: MSC Software develops simulation software technology that enables engineers to validate and optimize their designs using virtual prototypes. Customers in almost every part of manufacturing use our software to complement, and in some cases even replace the physical prototype “build and test” process that has traditionally been used in product design.

MSC Software collaborates with many universities and student teams all around the world. This is important for us, not only for driving the usage of our products, but also to support students doing efficient engineering work, using the same tools as in the industry. A perfect way to successfully execute projects at the university and to learn for the future!

Springing KTH Hyperloop
Into Action

Lesjöforskoncernen har funnits sedan 1675 och är idag en av världens ledande tillverkare av fjädrar samt stansade och pressade detaljer. Totalt består vi av ett femtiotal tillverkande och säljande bolag och finns över hela världen.

Teknik är en del av vårt DNA. Vi vill vara det självklara valet för våra kunder gällande hållbara tekniska lösningar inom vårt expertområde. Genom sponsring av Hyperloop och liknande studentprojekt vill vi främja samarbetet mellan ingenjörsstudenter och arbetslivet, ge teknisk stöttning i projektet och förhoppningsvis därigenom bidra till ökad kunskap kring fjädrande komponenter hos framtidens ingenjörer.
